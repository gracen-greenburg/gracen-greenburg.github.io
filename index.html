Problem Statement:

The bus system on USC's campus is difficult for students to navigate (especially for new students), and the Transloc app is often unhelpful in tracking buses.

Affinity Diagram: Bus-tling around Campus

There are many issues to consider when inspecting the TransLoc app. The majority of these can be split into different categories: Functionality (how the app should work), Interface (how to make the app more appealing / usable), Integration (how the app shoudl integrate with USofC specifically), Routes (how to improve the route system), and General Issues that cannot be categorized.
